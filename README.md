# Business Central Finance Speedrun

### A structured 5-day training accelerator for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central

---

## Overview

This self-paced training planner is designed for teams adopting **Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central** who need to build finance module fluency fast.

Traditional onboarding paths — week-long instructor-led courses, lengthy certification tracks — don't match the pace of modern digital transformation. Projects move quickly, and teams need to contribute from day one.

**The BC Finance Speedrun closes that gap.** It distils the most critical Business Central finance knowledge into a focused 5-day sprint at approximately 2 hours per day, with hands-on checkpoints and real-world conversation prompts at every stage.

---

## Who is this for?

| Audience | Benefit |
|:---------|:--------|
| **New team members** joining an active BC implementation | Ramp up without slowing the project down |
| **Consultants** crossing over from Dynamics 365 CE, Power Platform, or other ERP systems | Build BC finance fluency and speak the language in client workshops from week one |
| **Project teams** kicking off a Business Central adoption | A single checklist ensures foundational concepts aren't skipped — avoiding costly rework later |
| **Finance staff** transitioning from legacy systems | Understand where familiar processes live inside Business Central |

---

## Training Schedule

| Day | Module | Key Topics |
|:---:|:-------|:-----------|
| **1** | Foundations and Mental Model | Chart of Accounts, Posting Groups, Dimensions, transaction flow |
| **2** | Sub-Ledgers | Accounts Receivable, Accounts Payable, Bank, Cash, payment application |
| **3** | Compliance and Reporting | GST/BAS, period-end close, Financial Reports, Power BI |
| **4** | Dimensions, Budgets and Projects | Dimension modelling, G/L Budgets, Jobs/Projects, WIP recognition |
| **5** | Integration and Real-World Patterns | NFP considerations, BC-Dataverse sync, CE integration touchpoints |

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- Access to a **Business Central sandbox environment** (a free trial works)
- A modern web browser (Edge, Chrome, Safari, or Firefox)

### Launch the Planner

1. Open **`BCquicktraining.html`** in your browser.
2. The page automatically adapts to your device's **light or dark mode** preference.
3. Work through each day's checklist — your progress is saved locally in the browser.
4. Use the **Conversation Cheat Sheet** at the bottom to practise real implementation questions.

---

## Design Principles

- **Speedrun mindset** — learn the 20% of concepts that unlock 80% of finance conversations.
- **Hands-on first** — every day includes sandbox exercises, not just reading.
- **Conversation-ready** — each module ends with the questions you should be able to ask (and answer) in a real workshop.
- **Zero install** — one HTML file, no dependencies, works offline.
